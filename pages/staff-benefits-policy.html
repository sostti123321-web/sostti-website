Policy Statement

SOS Technical Training Institute values its staff as the most important asset in achieving our educational mission. This benefits policy is designed to attract, retain, and motivate qualified professionals by offering a comprehensive and competitive benefits package that supports their professional growth, financial security, and overall well-being.

Financial Benefits

Compensation Structure

  • Competitive salary scales based on qualifications and experience
  • Annual performance-based increments
  • Market adjustment reviews every two years
  • Overtime compensation at 1.5x hourly rate
  • Special allowances for technical certifications

Bonuses & Incentives

  • Annual performance bonus (up to one month's salary)
  • Eid bonus equivalent to one month's basic salary
  • Special achievement awards for exceptional performance
  • Long-service awards after 5, 10, 15, and 20 years
  • Research and publication incentives

Health & Wellness Benefits

Benefit Coverage Eligibility

Professional Development

Training & Development

  • Annual training budget for each staff member
  • Sponsorship for professional certifications
  • Conference and workshop attendance support
  • Higher education assistance program
  • Internal knowledge sharing sessions

Career Advancement

  • Clear career progression pathways
  • Internal promotion preference
  • Mentorship programs
  • Leadership development initiatives
  • Cross-functional training opportunities

Retirement Benefits

  • Provident Fund contribution (10% of basic salary matched by institute)
  • Gratuity payment after 5 years of continuous service
  • Retirement planning workshops
  • Pension scheme for eligible staff
  • Retirement gift and farewell ceremony

Additional Benefits

Work-Life Balance

  • Flexible working hours where possible
  • Remote work options for eligible positions
  • Family day events and social gatherings
  • Staff recreation room facilities
  • Subsidized meal services

Recognition & Rewards

  • Employee of the Month/Year awards
  • Service anniversary celebrations
  • Excellence in teaching awards
  • Innovation and initiative recognition
  • Peer recognition programs

Application Process

  • Benefits enrollment during onboarding process
  • Annual benefits review and update opportunity
  • HR department assistance for benefits queries
  • Online benefits portal for easy access
  • Confidential counseling for benefits decisions

Policy Amendments

The institute reserves the right to amend, modify, or discontinue any benefit described in this policy. Staff will be given reasonable notice of any significant changes to their benefits package.
