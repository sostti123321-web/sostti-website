Aims & Objectives

The specific goals and targets of SOS Technical Training Institute

Our Primary Aim

SOS Technical Training Institute (INFAQ Foundation Campus) aims at providing a high standard of technical and vocational training to SOS children, students of Korangi Academy and other under privileged and deserving boys and girls as well as the community at large to meet the entry level needs of the job market in their chosen technical field.

After acquiring technical education, students will be in a position to secure gainful employment to support their families and live in dignity to become good citizens of Pakistan. Also by joining the pool of skilled manpower the students will be contributing to the economic progress and prosperity of Pakistan.

Who We Serve

  • SOS Children: Residents of SOS Children's Villages
  • Korangi Academy Students: Students from our partner institution
  • Underprivileged Youth: Deserving boys and girls from marginalized communities
  • Community at Large: Wider community members seeking technical skills

Our Core Objectives

  • Quality Technical Programs: Offer comprehensive and industry-relevant technical programs that meet national and international standards.
  • Experienced Instructors: Provide instructors who have extensive industry experience and practical knowledge.
  • Balanced Education: Provide both theoretical knowledge and hands-on applications.
  • Real Workplace Simulation: Provide training in workshops that simulate actual workplace environments.
  • Modern Equipment: Provide up-to-date tools and equipment for students to practice with.
  • Industry Collaboration: Work closely with business and industry to establish and update curricula.
  • Professional Development: Provide educational training in work readiness skills and work ethics.
  • Internship Opportunities: Assist students in finding on-the-job internships.
  • Job Placement Support: Provide students with job search skills and assistance with job placements.
