Institute Overview

SOS Technical Training Institute (SOSTTI) is a premier vocational training institute committed to providing quality technical education to youth from underprivileged backgrounds. Inaugurated as a joint venture between SOS Children's Villages of Sindh and INFAQ Foundation.
