Policy Statement

SOS Technical Training Institute is committed to providing a safe and healthy environment for all students, staff, visitors, and contractors. We believe that safety is a shared responsibility and that every individual has the right to work and learn in an environment free from hazards.

Objectives

  • Prevent accidents, injuries, and occupational illnesses
  • Comply with all relevant health and safety legislation
  • Promote safety awareness and responsibility among all stakeholders
  • Provide adequate safety training and resources
  • Continuously improve our safety management system

Responsibilities

Management Responsibilities:

  • Provide and maintain safe equipment and facilities
  • Ensure adequate safety training for all staff and students
  • Investigate all accidents and incidents
  • Regularly review and update safety procedures

Staff Responsibilities:

  • Follow all safety procedures and instructions
  • Use personal protective equipment as required
  • Report hazards, accidents, and near-misses immediately
  • Participate in safety training and meetings

Student Responsibilities:

  • Follow safety instructions from instructors
  • Use equipment only after proper training
  • Report any unsafe conditions to staff
  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment

Emergency Procedures

  • Emergency evacuation plans are posted throughout the facility
  • Regular fire drills are conducted
  • First aid kits are available in all workshops and labs
  • Trained first aid personnel are available during institute hours
  • Emergency contact numbers are displayed prominently

Hazard Identification and Control

Regular safety inspections are conducted to identify and control hazards in the following areas:

  • Electrical safety
  • Machine guarding
  • Fire safety
  • Ergonomics
  • Workshop safety
