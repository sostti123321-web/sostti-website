Policy Statement

SOS Technical Training Institute is committed to the proper management, maintenance, and utilization of all institutional assets. This policy ensures that assets are acquired, maintained, and disposed of in a manner that maximizes their value and supports the institute's educational mission.

Asset Classification

Fixed Assets

  • Land and buildings
  • Machinery and equipment
  • Furniture and fixtures
  • Vehicles
  • Computer hardware and servers

Movable Assets

  • Office equipment
  • Teaching aids
  • Laboratory instruments
  • Library resources
  • Sports equipment

Intangible Assets

  • Software licenses
  • Digital resources
  • Intellectual property
  • Brand and reputation

Maintenance & Repairs

  • Preventive maintenance schedules for all equipment
  • Regular calibration of measuring instruments
  • Prompt reporting of equipment malfunctions
  • Authorized technicians only for repairs
  • Maintenance logs maintained for critical assets

Asset Utilization

  • Assets used only for institute purposes
  • Proper training required before using specialized equipment
  • Booking system for shared resources
  • Optimal utilization monitored regularly
  • Assets not to be removed from premises without authorization

Asset Disposal

  • Disposal committee approves asset write-offs
  • Obsolete assets disposed through transparent process
  • Environmentally responsible disposal methods
  • Data destruction for IT equipment before disposal
  • Proceeds from sale recorded in institute accounts

Insurance & Risk Management

  • Comprehensive insurance for high-value assets
  • Regular risk assessment of asset vulnerabilities
  • Security measures for valuable equipment
  • Backup systems for critical digital assets
  • Disaster recovery plan for asset protection

Responsibilities

Asset Manager:

  • Maintain comprehensive asset register
  • Coordinate maintenance schedules
  • Conduct regular asset audits
  • Process asset disposal requests

Department Heads:

  • Ensure proper use of departmental assets
  • Report maintenance requirements
  • Monitor asset utilization
  • Approve internal asset transfers

All Staff:

  • Use assets responsibly and carefully
  • Report damage or malfunction immediately
  • Follow asset usage procedures
  • Participate in asset verification
