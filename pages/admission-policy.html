Policy Statement

SOS Technical Training Institute is committed to providing equal opportunities for admission to all eligible candidates regardless of gender, religion, ethnicity, or socioeconomic background. Our admission process is designed to be transparent, fair, and merit-based.

General Admission Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 - 35 years
  • Basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Successful completion of admission test/interview
  • Submission of required documents
  • Payment of admission fee

Admission Procedure

  • Obtain admission form from institute office
  • Complete the form with accurate information
  • Attach required documents (CNIC/B-form, educational certificates, photos)
  • Submit form before deadline
  • Appear for admission test/interview

Required Documents

  • Matriculation/Middle certificate
  • CNIC/B-Form copy
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Father's/Guardian's CNIC copy

Fee Structure & Payment

  • Admission fee must be paid at the time of registration
  • The fee will be paid in a lump sum
  • Late payment may result in fine or cancellation of admission
  • Fee concession available for deserving students
  • Refund policy as per institute regulations

Admission Cancellation & Refund

  • Admission may be cancelled due to disciplinary issues
  • Fee refund will be processed as per institute policy
  • Written application required for withdrawal
  • No refund after one month of course commencement
