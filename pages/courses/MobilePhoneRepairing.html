Mobile Phone Repairing

NAVTTC-recognized technical training at SOS Technical Training Institute, Karachi.

Course Overview

The Mobile Phone Repairing Course provides hands-on training in diagnosing and repairing hardware and software issues in smartphones and tablets.

What You Will Learn

  • Use of Multimeter
  • All Mobile Jack Replacement
  • LCD Panel Replacement
  • IC Replacement
  • Mobile Assembling / Disassembling
  • Jumpering Tricks
  • Security / Pattern Unlocking
  • All Mobile Flashing

Career Opportunities

  • Mobile Software Technician
  • Service Center Technician
  • Customer Support Technician
  • Mobile Accessories Specialist
  • Refurbished Device Technician
