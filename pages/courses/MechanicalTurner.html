Mechanical Turner

NAVTTC-recognized technical training at SOS Technical Training Institute, Karachi.

Course Overview

The Mechanical Turner Course provides comprehensive training in precision machining using conventional lathes and related machine tools. Students learn to read technical drawings, measure tolerances, and produce accurate components.

What You Will Learn

  • Maintain Safe Work Environment
  • Carry Out Bench Work
  • Perform Grinding Operations
  • Perform Shaping Operations
  • Perform Turning Operations

Career Opportunities

Graduates of this course can pursue various professional roles, including:

  • Mechanical Turner
  • Lathe Machine Operator
  • Machinist
  • Maintenance Technician
  • Workshop Assistant
  • Tool Room Technician
  • Production Machine Operator
  • Self-Employed Turner / Machining Contractor
